How the 7-Day Scanning Window Works:

  • Today's Events: Always take priority and show as "📅 Today: [Event Name]"
  • Past Events (1-7 days ago): Available for backfilling, show as "🔄 Backfill: [Event Name] ([X]d ago)"
  • Expired Events (>7 days ago): Not scannable, show as "⏰ Expired: [Event Name] ([X]d ago)"
  • Future Events: Not yet scannable, show as "⏳ Next: [Event Name] ([X]d)"
  • No Events: Show as "❌ No events created"
